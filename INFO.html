TRAP INDUSTRIES

This is a company that appeared in 2023 and works on 3D design and programming.

The company is currently working on a project called FUTURE ILLUSION.

FUTURE ILLUSION - is a game in which the main character will return to his mechanical creations, which he tried to destroy due to a failed experiment. However, for an unknown reason, someone restores and protects everything. The main character will have to investigate.
